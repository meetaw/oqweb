The kelvi-veri begins




The Empire Strikes Back was more fun than A New Hope. The Return of the King won nine more Oscars than The Two Towers. The Dark Knight was better than Batman Begins.

Continuing this rich tradition of sequels which top the originals is the IITM Open Quiz 2012, now with more fun (TM)!

It has long been one of the fixtures of every quizzer’s calendar, something that excites everyone from wide-eyed school kids to college-goers to jaded software professionals. Organised and set by the Quiz Club of IIT Madras, it is a product of the passionate and competitive culture of quizzing in campus. For years the finals have been the battlegrounds of the best quizzers in the region, and this time you could be one of them.

If you feel that you don’t get enough opportunities to show off your knowledge of the family tree of the current president of Azerbaijan or the filmography of François Truffaut or anything else under the sun for that matter, make a beeline for the tropical evergreen forests in Adyar on October 28th.

We invented the LVC. Expect more.

Date: October 28th, 2012
Time: 14:00 hrs
Venue:Students Activities Centre, IIT Madras

50 days remaining
