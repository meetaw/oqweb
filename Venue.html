Venue




The IITM Open Quiz will be held at the Students Activites Centre(SAC), IIT Madras on the 8th of January, 2012 from 1:00 P.M. onwards


How to Get to IIT Madras:


IIT Madras is located on Sardar Patel Road in Adyar, next to the Cancer Institute (near the Guindy Snake Park) and opposite the Central Leather Research Institute. A short guide on how to reach the Main Gate of IIT Madras is provided for your information:

  • From Chennai Central Railway Station: From Chennai Central, the quickest way to IIT Madras is by a local train. Trains can be caught from the Park Town station,which is just across from Central. Take a train on the Velachery lineto Kasturbai Nagar station, from where the Main Gate is a five minute walk. Alternatively, a bus can be caught from Central to Saidapet and from Saidapet any bus towards Adyar will pass by the Central Leather Research Institute. Autos and Taxis are also available, for roughly Rs. 140 px-Rs. 250.

  • From Chennai Egmore Railway Station: The easiest way from Chennai Egmore is totravel on the 23C bus to the CLRI stop, which is across the road from the Main Gate. Taxis and autos are an alternative, for fares similar to those mentioned above. Local trains can be caught to Guindy, fromwhere share autos and autos are availabe to reach the Main Gate.

  • From CMBT: Catch the 23M bus from CMBT to CLRI stop which is opposite the IIT Madras Main Gate. Alternatively taxis and autos are available, with fares usually in the range of Rs.200 to Rs. 300.

  • From Airport: A train to Guindy followed by an auto to the Main Gate is the quickest way to reach IIT Madras. Prepaid taxis and autos are available at the airport with fixed fares.

Once at the Main Gate, you can use the shuttle service provided. There will be buses every 15 to 20 minutes. Get off at the central circle, called Gajendra circle, where volunteers will guide you towards the Students Activities Centre(SAC). If you are arriving in a vehicle, proceed along either of Delhi or Bonn avenues to Gajendra circle, from where our volunteers will guide you to the parking areas.
